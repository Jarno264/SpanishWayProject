Cologne

Cologne is a vibrant city in western Germany, known for its stunning Gothic cathedral, the Kölner Dom, which dominates the skyline. Located on the Rhine River, it blends rich history with modern culture, offering world-class museums, a lively arts scene, and a strong local identity shaped by centuries of trade and tradition.

Münster

Münster is a historic city in western Germany, known for its charming old town, vibrant student population, and rich medieval heritage. It blends tradition with modern life, making it a dynamic and livable city with strong cultural and academic roots.

Haus Kemnade

Haus Kemnade is a moated castle near Bochum, known for its picturesque Renaissance architecture and peaceful riverside setting. Once a noble residence, it now hosts exhibitions and concerts, preserving the charm of medieval Westphalia.

Haus Bodelschwingh

Located in Dortmund, Haus Bodelschwingh is one of the region’s best-preserved moated castles. It remains privately owned and offers a glimpse into the aristocratic life of the Ruhr region during the 17th century.

Vischering Castle

Vischering Castle is a stunning example of medieval defense architecture. Surrounded by a moat and lush forests near Lüdinghausen, it houses a museum and offers insights into life in the Middle Ages.
