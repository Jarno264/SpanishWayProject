Vischering Castle

Vischering Castle is a medieval moated fortress in Lüdinghausen, Germany. Known for its well-preserved condition, round shape, and scenic surroundings, it’s a true gem of Westphalian heritage and one of the most picturesque castles in the country.

  • Medieval Design: Round layout, drawbridge, and defensive features give it authentic knightly charm.
  • Historical Museum: Hosts exhibits on local history, life in the Middle Ages, and castle architecture.
  • Scenic Setting: Surrounded by a wide moat, lush greenery, and walking trails, it offers postcard-perfect views.
  • Family Friendly: Interactive museum experiences and events make it great for visitors of all ages.
