Münster

Münster is a historic city in western Germany, known for its charming old town, vibrant student population, and rich medieval heritage. It blends tradition with modern life, making it a dynamic and livable city with strong cultural and academic roots.

  • Bicycle Capital: With more bikes than people, Münster is famous for its cycling-friendly infrastructure.
  • University City: Home to one of Germany’s largest universities, giving it a youthful and energetic atmosphere.
  • Historic Architecture: Features beautiful Gothic churches, the Prinzipalmarkt, and reconstructed medieval buildings.
  • Peace of Westphalia: Played a central role in ending the Thirty Years' War in 1648, shaping European history.
  • Green and Livable: Offers abundant parks, lakes, and eco-conscious urban planning, making it one of Germany’s most livable cities.
