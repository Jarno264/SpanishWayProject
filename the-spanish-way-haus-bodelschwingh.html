Haus Bodelschwingh

Haus Bodelschwingh is one of the most important moated castles in the Ruhr region, located in Dortmund. With its striking Renaissance style and centuries-old noble lineage, it is a rare example of a privately owned historic residence still in active use.

  • Renaissance Architecture: Characterized by decorative gables, corner towers, and intricate masonry.
  • Family Legacy: Still inhabited by descendants of the original noble family, preserving living history.
  • Park and Surroundings: Surrounded by a scenic English-style landscape garden and reflecting moats.
  • Exclusive Access: Visits are limited due to private ownership, adding an air of mystique to the site.
