Haus Kemnade

Haus Kemnade is a moated castle located near Bochum, Germany. With origins dating back to the 13th century, it stands as a beautiful example of Renaissance architecture and regional history.

  • Historic Setting: Surrounded by water and greenery, Haus Kemnade offers a peaceful retreat into medieval architecture.
  • Museum & Culture: The castle now hosts exhibitions on musical instruments, local history, and temporary cultural events.
  • Preserved Architecture: Its thick stone walls, towers, and drawbridge evoke the atmosphere of a traditional Westphalian manor.
  • Popular Excursion Spot: It is a favorite destination for locals and tourists alike, offering scenic walks and cultural enrichment.
